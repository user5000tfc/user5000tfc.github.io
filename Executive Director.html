Agim Nuhiu

Executive Director

Professor of Law; Former Minister of Interior.

Profile

Agim Nuhiu was born on August 24, 1977 in Tetovo. He is a full professor at the Faculty of Law at the University of Tetovo. Between 2016 and 2017 he served in senior positions in the Ministries of Interior and Justice, including serving as Minister of Interior (29 December 2016 – 1 June 2017). He has held multiple academic and administrative roles in higher education and public institutions and contributed to national commissions and policy formation. He has been active in legal research, university leadership and public service.

Other

  • Former member of State Commissions for exams and anti-discrimination.
  • Experience as Deputy Minister in several ministries.
